SOMA Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What we collect

SOMA stores all personal data — your name, diary entries, memories, and AI conversations — exclusively on your device. Nothing is uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly create an account.

If you create an account, we store: your email address and an encrypted password. We never store your conversation content on our servers.

2. What we never do

  • We never sell your data to anyone.
  • We never read your diary or AI conversations.
  • We never share your information with advertisers.
  • We never use your data to train AI models.

3. AI conversations

When you chat with Soma, your messages are sent to Groq (our AI provider) to generate a response, then immediately discarded. Groq processes requests in memory and does not store conversation history. See Groq's privacy policy.

4. Intimacy notes and matching

If you choose to share intimacy notes for dating matching, these are transmitted directly and securely between matched users' devices only — SOMA never reads or stores them.

5. Trusted contact

Your trusted contact's name and phone number are stored on your device only and are never uploaded.

6. Data deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes everything from your device and, if you have an account, from our servers.

7. Children

SOMA is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@soma.app
